Rexburg Idaho Temple

Sitting at the highest point of Rexburg the beutiful Rexburg Idaho Temple can be seen at any time of the day or night. This building is a truly iconinc part of the community.

Map of Rexburg

The city of Rexburg is a small farming community that sits in southeastern Idaho in the Snake River plane. The population is currently at 35,300; which has nearly doubled since the year 2000, when it was just under 18,000 residents.

Teton Mountain Range

Rexburg boasts amazing views of the Teton Mountain Range on a nearly daily basis. These mountains are a great place to hike, ski, bike, fish, and many more great outdoor activities. This Mountain range provides the locals and visitors plenty of opportunity to experience nature.

Rexburg Watertower

The Iconing Watertower Displays the Towns name while also being a symbol of the towns history and legacy. Rexburg has been on the map and growing for along time and this landmark has been with it every step of the way.

Brigham Young University - Idaho

There is not dispute about how influential Brigham Young University - Idaho (Formerly Ricks College) has been in the Growth of this small Farm Community. Over the years the university has blossomed into a full 4 year college and continues to see higher enrolment numbers with each passing year. This school has brought a new life to a once quiet town, along with views from different countries around the world.
