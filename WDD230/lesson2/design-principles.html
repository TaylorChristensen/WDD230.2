Visual Hieratchy

Scoutium

Scoutium.com

Scoutium does a great job at integrating Visual Hieratchy into their web page by highlighting the icons in the bright green circles. By doing this ontop of the existing dark blue backround the icons stand out to the users eye. Another thing they have applied well is the headings that are bolded over the paragraphs next to the icons.

Hick's Law

Apple

Apple.com

Apple has done t again. They applied Hick's Law right off the bat with the two options they provided overlaying the image. They provide the user with two links to continue to other pages; the links are titled "Learn More" and "Buy". This applied Hick's Law becasue they are assuming that the user will want to do one of these two things upon cisiting the first page of the site.

Rule of Thirds

Staak

Staack.co.uk

The rule of third is displayed by Staak on their desktop web interface in a horizontal plane. This is where they place the main focal point/ object of the image on one of the grid lines that measures the image for use of this rule. On their mobile app they display the use of this rule placing the words in the bottom third of the image while gilling the top two thirds with the image portion of the graphic.
